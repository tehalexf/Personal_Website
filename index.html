About Me

Howdy howdy howdy!

I'm a software engineer technical lead @ Audible on the purchasing experience team, and I'm currently located in the greater New York City area. There's just something about the bagels and the pizza!

I love making things from scratch (even if it exists already) so I can learn how it works, how to improve it, and how to break it!

Drawing inspiration from things that exist is nothing to be ashamed of, and in my experience it is very rewarding to figure out how others solved the same problem.

There is just something so empowering about computer science -- you're fully empowered to create a website or an app using only your own imagination and hard work. The startup cost is low and accessible to anyone who wants to learn!

I try to share this love of learning and building with others through tutoring and teaching, and it is a pleasure seeing the spark of excitement in a student's eyes as they figure out something for the first time! Teaching others what I've learned is fun too -- some of the people I've tutored think I'm wild and energetic, but actually I just really enjoy explaining computer science.

A lot of my projects are private, but some of my old code and projects are open source.

In my free time, I enjoy making apps for games I play (satisgraphtory.com) and playing Overwatch & misc base building games.
