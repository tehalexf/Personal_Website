About Me

I chose the name "tehalexf" ever since I've had an online presence. Strangely enough there is someone (or someones!) out there who use this name as well...ah well!

Hailing from South California, It's a little weird to be living in the other half of the state!

I'm a student at UC Davis and UC Berkeley, and I love to code! My philosophy has always been to make things from scratch -- even if it exists already -- so I can learn how it works, how to improve it, and how to break it!

In my free time I like to cook, tutor, play the violin, and think about my next projects!

You might know me as the Tutoring Chair for Computer Science at UC Davis, or "The Piazza God" at both Berkeley or Davis. If this is true I hope you have learned something from my help!

A lot of my projects are private, but some of my old code and projects are open source -- I admit I use print statements more than I'd like but after learning to use a debugger, I can say I'm much better at debugging in general, using these two tricks combined.

I like to know a lot about lots of stuff -- learning is my passion. Teaching others what I've learned is fun too -- some of the people I've tutored think I'm wild and energetic, but I really enjoy explaining computer science.

In any case, welcome to my site! Enjoy your stay!
