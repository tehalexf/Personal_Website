About Me

I'm a student at UC Davis and UC Berkeley, and I love to code! My philosophy has always been to make things from scratch -- even if it exists already -- so I can learn how it works, how to improve it, and how to break it!

In my free time I like to tutor, play the violin, and design new projects!

You might know me as the Tutoring Chair for the Computer Science Club at UC Davis, or "The Piazza God" at both Berkeley and Davis. If you do, I hope you learned something with my help!

A lot of my projects are private, but some of my old code and projects are open source -- I admit I use print statements more than I'd like -- but some things you just can't do with a debugger.

I like to know a lot about lots of stuff -- learning is my passion. Teaching others what I've learned is fun too -- some of the people I've tutored think I'm wild and energetic, but actually I just really enjoy explaining computer science.

In any case, welcome to my site! Enjoy your stay!
